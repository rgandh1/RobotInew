Short Description:
===================
This iSig TC simulates a roaming MTC of 30s duration to the callsSubscriber() 
(as B-Party) being in a foreign location and released by the A-Party.

Flow details:
==============
- After session initiation and call establishment (answer) the 
  TC waits 30s to simulate the ongoing chat. 
- Then the TC reports to IN that the A-Party released the call 
- This is (or should be) responded by IN with a final release 
  meaning that the MTC leg is finished. 

Parameters: 
============
  mscAddress         of the B-Party (callSubscriber()) 
  roamingVlrAddress  of the B-Party's current (foreign) location
  mtcAPartyMsisdn
