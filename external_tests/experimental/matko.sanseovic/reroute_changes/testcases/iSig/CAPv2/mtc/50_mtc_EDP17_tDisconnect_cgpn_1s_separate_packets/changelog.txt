Short Description:
===================
This iSig TC simulates a (non-roaming) MTC of 1s duration to the callsSubscriber() 
(as B-Party) being in the home location and released by the A-Party.

Flow details:
==============
- After session initiation and call establishment (answer) the 
  TC waits 1s to simulate the ongoing chat. 
- Then the TC reports to IN that the A-Party released the call 
- This is (or should be) responded by IN with a final release 
  meaning that the MTC leg is finished. 

Parameters: 
============
  mscAddress         of the B-Party (callSubscriber()) 
  mtcAPartyMsisdn
